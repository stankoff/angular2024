Who we are

The DESIGN TEAM was established in 2004 with the ambition of creating an "in-house agency” approach to the design needs of the various companies within Clunetech.

We strive to deliver top quality design-thinking, and we enjoy the huge variety of work that we are involved in day-to-day, whether it’s a video for internal distribution or 3d visualisations of our new offices and creative spaces.

We are a close-knit bunch of creatives who love what we do. We aim for original, unexpected, eclectic, rebellious and sophisticated outcomes - always on the edge of commercial reality and cultural interest. Because the ultimate goal is happy clients.

Get in touch. Let’s discuss how we can help with your upcoming projects.
